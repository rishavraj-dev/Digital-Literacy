### Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

**DO:**
1. **Do set personal accounts to private** to control who can view your daily life and photos.
2. **Do Google your own name** occasionally to understand what a recruiter or professor sees when they search for you.
3. **Do share your academic achievements,** on Linkdin , post about the hackathon participation and wins 
4. **Do think before you post;** as screenshot and screenrecording can be with someone even though your post is deleted
5. **Do engage positively** by uplifting peers and sharing useful technical resources.

**DON'T:**
1. **Don't share sensitive personal information** for example home address, phone number, or current live location publicly.
2. **Don't post unprofessional or offensive content,** as Future employers can see the digital footprint
3. **Don't use social media to complain** publicly about professors, university administration, or specific classmates. 
4. **Don't accept connection requests from suspicious accounts** who have no mutual connections.
5. **Don't engage in internet drama or cyberbullying;** maintain a respectful and neutral tone in all online interactions.
